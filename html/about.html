ABOUT US

Kowealth Properties is a real estate firm working to address Ghana's most pressing housing issues. We operate with the primary goal of obtaining the highest possible customer service standards.

Our mission is to contribute to the improvement of Ghanaians' and West Africans' quality of life by implementing sustainable development principles in the provision of affordable housing in a safe and secure environment.

Ghana currently has a social housing problem, with a deficit of 1.7 million units and a minimum of 170,000 housing units would have to be built annually.

OUR VISION

To set the standard in the real estate business for the delivery of real estate services and projects. Kowealth Properties' objective is to modernize and advance the experience of building, remodeling, purchasing and selling.

Kowealth Properties started out with the goal of becoming the country's top real estate firm, providing exclusive properties and striving to be the company with the most delighted customers.
